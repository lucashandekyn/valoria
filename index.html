Realms of Valoria

Welcome to the world of Valoria, a land of magic and adventure.

About the Campaign

The realm of Valoria is a land of magic and wonder, where brave heroes rise to face the challenges of a dangerous world. From the frozen tundras of the north to the burning sands of the south, Valoria is home to a wide variety of cultures and creatures, each with their own unique history and traditions.

Join us as we embark on an epic journey through the realm of Valoria, a land filled with danger and intrigue at every turn. With powerful magic at your fingertips and brave companions by your side, you'll face fearsome foes and uncover ancient mysteries as you fight to protect the realm from the forces of darkness.

The Characters

Meet the heroes of our campaign:

  • Aelinor, the elven ranger - Aelinor is a skilled tracker and archer, able to move stealthily through the wilderness and take down her enemies from a distance. She is fiercely independent and fiercely loyal to her friends.
  • Garrick, the human paladin - Garrick is a holy warrior, devoted to the cause of good and sworn to protect the innocent. He wields a powerful enchanted sword and can call upon the power of his deity to heal his allies and smite his enemies.
  • Kriv, the dwarven rogue - Kriv is a cunning and agile thief, skilled at sneaking and stealing. He is always on the lookout for a good deal, and is not above a little bit of thievery if it means getting ahead.
  • Nara, the half-elf sorcerer - Nara is a powerful spellcaster, able to wield the elements and call upon ancient magic to defeat her foes. She is quick-witted and always ready with a clever plan.
  • Thorne, the dragonborn barbarian - Thorne is a fierce warrior

The Quest

The heroes have been called upon by the King of Valoria to investigate a series of strange occurrences in the kingdom. Villages have been attacked by unknown creatures, and dark magic has been sensed in the area. The heroes must journey to the kingdom's borders to uncover the source of the disturbances and put a stop to them before they threaten the safety of the entire realm.

  • Aelinor will use her tracking skills to locate the source of the attacks.
  • Garrick will use his holy powers to protect the group and purify any dark magic they encounter.
  • Kriv will use his stealth and thievery skills to gather information and sneak past any obstacles.
  • Nara will use her magic to defend the group and uncover any hidden secrets.
  • Thorne will use his brute strength and combat skills to defeat any foes that stand in their way.

Can the heroes complete their quest and save the kingdom from the darkness that threatens it? Only time will tell...
